Say goodbye to outdated digital signage,
say hello to Slide Schedule Pro!

A standalone simple digital signage app where you can schedule a slide for multiple presentation times. No more outdated or duplicate slides in multiple shows.

Download See it in action

Features

Open file format
Project files are saved as .json text, so you can modify them with any text editor.
High quality scaling
Images are scaled are interpolated at the highest quality possible.
Any resolution
There is no limit to your output resolution - only by your video card capabilities.
Auto-save
Project changes are auto-saved in the event of a catostrophic failure.
Save revisioning
You can enable revisioning so previous saves are not overwritten.
Override Duration
Some slides may need to stay on the screen for a longer duration than others. You can easily manage override settings on per-slide basis.
