Slide Schedule Pro


Our first app came out of a need to schedule slides for different days and times of the week. We became frustrated loading one show for Sunday, then stopping it to load up another slideshow for Wednesday - repeat every week. Don't forget to delete that old slide! Some slides were used in both presentations - don't forget to delete both! There had to be a better way.

We looked far and wide for an app that met our needs - nothing. So we decided to write our own.


Slide Schedule Pro lets you import an image as a slide, and schedule it any day, any time - you can expire it too! Click the link below to check it out.
